BELCORO KNITTING YARNS

The greater stability of the Belcoro yarns to withstand deformation forces means that, when the tubular knit fabrics pass through the dyeing machine-with the dye liquor and the fabric being in constant motion-they have a far lower tendency to become tangled and knotted than tubular knitted fabrics made of ring yarn. This stability encourages a good finished fabric appearance. Belcoro knits provide a superb print-prepared fabric owing to the clear fabric appearance.

INDIGO DYED YARNS

With a present production capacity of 1,00,000 kgs per month, we manufacture a variety of Indigo Dyed Yarns in the count range from 6s to 60s; in both Carded and Combed qualities; in single and multiply; that are most suitable for High quality Shirtings, Bottoms and Children Garments.

BAMBOO YARNS

Bamboo owns an unique anti-bacteria and bacteriostatic bio-agent named "bamboo kun". It is scientifically proved that even after fifty times of washing, bamboo fiber possesses excellent function of anti-bacteria.
